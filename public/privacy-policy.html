Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Skill Shift and Farrey ("we", "our", or "us") respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you use Skill Shift and Farrey, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Skills and interests
  • WhatsApp number (if provided and verified)
  • Messages and conversation metadata
  • Session booking information
  • Notes, documents, and educational content uploaded by users
  • Ratings, endorsements, comments, and reviews
  • Device information and usage analytics
  • Push notification tokens

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Enable messaging and collaboration features
  • Facilitate mentoring and session bookings
  • Verify WhatsApp contact information
  • Provide recommendations and personalized experiences
  • Enable note sharing and educational features through Farrey
  • Send notifications and reminders
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, and misuse of the platform

AI Features

Farrey may use AI-powered features such as note summaries, flashcards, quizzes, roadmap generation, study planning, and contextual learning assistance. Information submitted for these features may be processed by third-party AI service providers solely for generating requested results.

Data Storage

User data is securely stored using Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging

Third-Party Services

The application may use services provided by:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Gemini AI
  • Google Play Services
  • WhatsApp (for optional user-initiated communication)

Sharing of Information

We do not sell user data. Information may be shared only when necessary to provide app functionality, comply with legal obligations, or protect platform security.

User Content

Users are responsible for the content they upload, share, message, or publish through Skill Shift and Farrey. We reserve the right to remove content that violates our policies.

Notifications

We may send push notifications related to messages, sessions, endorsements, reminders, updates, and other app-related activities. Users can manage notification preferences through device settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children's Privacy

The application is intended for students and users aged 13 years or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion by contacting us. Upon verification, reasonable efforts will be made to remove associated personal data.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
